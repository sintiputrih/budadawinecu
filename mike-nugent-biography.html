Who is Mike Nugent?

Mike Nugent is a famous sports celebrity. He is an American Footballer and plays as a kicker.

He currently plays for Cincinnati Bengals. Mike was recognized twice as a consensus All-American. He is an attacking player and very good in his game when he is on the field.

Mike Nugent: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Mike was born in Centerville, Ohio, the U.S.A. on March 2, 1982. He holds an American nationality and a North American ethnicity.

His birth name is Michael Nugent. Mike’s father’s name is Dan Nugent and his mother’s name is Carolyn Nugent.

His childhood was spent in the place he was born. Mike’s talent in football was seen from a very young age.

Mike Nugent: Education History

Mike Nugent attended Centerville High School for his high school education. Apart from his studies, he was very good at football and played as a kicker on the school’s team. Later he went to Ohio State University.

He was given a good opportunity by his college to play football. Mike also became a top college placekicker in 2004.
