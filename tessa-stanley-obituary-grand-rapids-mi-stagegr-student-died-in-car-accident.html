Tessa Stanley Obituary, Grand Rapids MI StageGR Student Died In Car Accident

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, February 1, 2024

Tessa Stanley Obituary, Death Cause – We are saddened to inform you of the unexpected departure of Tessa Stanley, a treasured member of the StageGR family. It is with heavy hearts and great regret that we make this announcement. In a devastating turn of events, Tessa, a student who was both talented and energetic, passed away this evening, leaving our community in a state of shock and grief.

Tessa’s appearance on stage was a testament to her enthusiasm for the performing arts, as she participated in a number of different performances. She had crucial parts in numerous noteworthy performances, including “Finding Nemo” as Nemo, “Addams Family” where she represented Wednesday Addams, “High School Musical” as Kelsi, and provided her talents to “Seussical” and “101 Dalmatians” productions in support staff roles.

All of those who had the honour of working alongside Tessa were left with a lasting impression as a result of her unwavering commitment, creative spirit, and friendly personality, which made her an indispensable component of the StageGR experience. In the process of coming to grips with the passing of such a brilliant and young talent, the community of StageGR is struggling with a tremendous sense of great sadness.

Both Tessa’s enthusiasm for the performing arts and her dedication to her profession shone through in each and every performance, serving as a source of motivation for her fellow cast members and leaving an indelible mark on the audience. The community of StageGR would like to extend an invitation to people from all walks of life to join them on December 12, 2023, from 3:30 to 6:00 pm, at East Church. This is a gesture of solidarity and remembering.
