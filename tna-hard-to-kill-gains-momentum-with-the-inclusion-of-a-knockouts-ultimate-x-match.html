TNA Hard to Kill Gains Momentum with the Inclusion of a Knockouts Ultimate X Match

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 10, 2024

TNA Wrestling is set to bring back the Knockouts Ultimate X match for the Hard to Kill event on January 13, 2024.

This marks only the second time in the company’s history that the Knockouts will compete in an Ultimate X match. The event will take place at The Palms in Las Vegas and features an updated lineup, including:

  • TNA World Championship: Alex Shelley (c) vs. Moose
  • TNA Knockouts World Championship: Trinity (c) vs. Jordynne Grace
  • X Division Championship: Chris Sabin (c) vs. El Hijo del Vikingo vs. KUSHIDA
  • Knockouts Ultimate X: Participants TBD

The return of the Knockouts Ultimate X match promises to be an exciting addition to the Hard to Kill event, offering fans a unique and thrilling contest featuring some of TNA’s top female talent.
