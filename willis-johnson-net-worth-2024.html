Who is Willis Johnson?

Willis Johnson is the founder of Copart, Inc., a global leader in online vehicle auctions. His entrepreneurial spirit and innovative approach to the salvage vehicle industry have been the cornerstone of his success. Johnson’s journey from a single salvage yard to a multinational corporation is a testament to his business acumen and vision.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Johnson’s story began in California, where he was born and raised. From an early age, he was exposed to the world of business, as his family owned a small salvage yard. This early exposure laid the foundation for what would become a lifelong career in the automotive industry.

The Rise of Copart, Inc.

In 1982, Johnson took a bold step and founded Copart with just one salvage yard in California. His innovative idea to computerize the inventory system revolutionized the way salvage yards operated, setting Copart apart from its competitors. Over the years, the company expanded its reach, both domestically and internationally, and transitioned into online auctions, which further propelled its growth.
