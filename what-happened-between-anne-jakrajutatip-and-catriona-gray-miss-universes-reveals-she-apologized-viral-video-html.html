Anne Jakrajutatip and Catriona Gray: A Surprising Apology

Anne Jakrajutatip, the owner of the Miss Universe Organization (MUO), made a shocking revelation during an interview on “Fast Talk with Boy Abunda.” She revealed that she had extended an apology to Catriona Gray for an incident that took place in Thailand in November 202During that event, Grey and Jakrajutatip were captured on camera engaged in a candid conversation. It’s worth mentioning that Clint Bondad, an actor-model who was once romantically linked to Grey, started dating Jakrajutatip after his separation from Grey.

Clearing the Air: Anne’s Apology

Anne Jakrajutatip chose this interview to address the past incident with Catriona Gray. She expressed her deep respect for Grey, stating that it was her first time meeting her.
