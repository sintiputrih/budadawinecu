Barry Keoghan Causes Scene At Hotel As Its Revealed He Broke Up With Long-Term Girlfriend

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Highlights

  • Barry Keoghan's personal life is experiencing chaos despite his success in his career, as he was asked to leave a Scottish hotel after allegedly bothering other guests while partying.
  • Barry had recently ended his long-term relationship with Alyson Kierans, and their split was reportedly due to arguments about his partying behavior and suspicions of him getting close to someone else.
  • Barry has been moving on from his relationship and has been seen on a date night with Sabrina Carpenter, who bears a resemblance to his ex-girlfriend. They were comfortable engaging in PDA in front of onlookers.

Barry Keoghan is currently enjoying the success of his movie Saltburn. But while his career is at a high, the actor is experiencing chaos in his personal life. Most recently, Barry caused a scene at a Scottish hotel after the news broke that he’d split ways with his long-term girlfriend, Alyson Kierans.

According to The Sun, Barry was staying at the Malmaison in Dundee, Scotland, where his rumored partying ways got the best of him. However, he was asked to leave the hotel after he allegedly started bothering other guests.

Barry Was Told To Leave After Bothering Hotel Guests

“Staff asked him to leave after he started misbehaving in front of other guests at the bar,” a source told the publication.

“He seemed in good spirits though and was trying to convince the bartenders that he had been in The Batman movie which he plays a brief role in and is uncredited for,” they continued.

“He was very proud of his part - and no one believed him which made it funny,” the insider added.

Barry and Alyson reportedly broke up last summer, only a year after welcoming their son. The actor was questioned about their break-up by GQ, but only had positive things to say about his ex. “She's [Alyson] done a great job, and she's an incredible mother,” Barry said.

The U.K. native also gushed over their son, Brando, adding, “I can’t get the little boy off my mind. It’s beautiful. Y’know, it’s crazy, but when he looks at you, you feel like the most important person in the world.”

“That’s the effect he has on me. He smiles at you and you’re like, ‘Wow, you're smiling at me like that? I don't deserve that, but anyways, thanks.’” Barry continued.

Barry And His Girlfriend Broke Up Over His Partying

When it was initially reported the couple broke up in July 2023, a source told The Sun that the decision came after repeated arguments about Barry’s partying. The report also alleged Alyson was uncomfortable with how close Barry had grown to their nurse.

“They’ve grown apart over recent months and things have finally come to a head,” the source alleged. “His partying caused arguments.”

“Alyson also suspects he has got close to someone else and is adamant there is no going back,” they went on. “As far as she’s concerned, it’s over.”

For now, Barry hasn’t addressed the allegations that he was asked to leave the Irish hotel. However, he has made it clear he’s moving on from his relationship, even if the dust has just settled. Barry has been seen hanging out with Sabrina Carpenter, who some fans have noticed has an uncanny resemblance to Alyson.

"It definitely seemed like a date,” an insider told PEOPLE after spotting Barry and Sabrina on a date night, noting the pair weren’t afraid to engage in PDA in front of onlookers.
