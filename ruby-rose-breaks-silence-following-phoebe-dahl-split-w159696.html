Ruby Rose Breaks Silence Following Phoebe Dahl Split

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 15, 2024

Nothing but love. Ruby Rose broke her silence via Twitter following her split from fiancee Phoebe Dahl on Monday, December 14. The Orange Is the New Black actress tweeted directly at the account for Dahl's clothing line, Faircloth & Supply.

"@FAIRCLOTHSUPPLY remains very dear to my heart. I will forever treasure our time together," Rose, 29, wrote. "I’m a better person because of all we shared."

Dahl, 27, replied: "You have brought me to my best and I will always love you. I can't wait to see what the future brings for us."

@FAIRCLOTHSUPPLY remains very dear to my heart. I will forever treasure our time together. I’m a better person because of all we shared.

— Ruby Rose (@RubyRose) December 15, 2015

Just hours earlier, Us Weekly broke the news that Rose and Dahl had split after two years of dating. The couple got engaged in March 2014.

"While we still love each other and support each other in every way, it is our mutual decision to part ways," Dahl told Us in a statement at the time. "Our break is not the result of any media speculation, and we want nothing but the best for each other. Thank you for respecting our privacy in this very difficult time."

@RubyRose you have brought me to my best and I will always love you. I can't wait to see what the future brings for us.

— Phoebe Dahl (@FAIRCLOTHSUPPLY) December 15, 2015

Dahl's rep also told Us that the designer had moved out of Rose's home in the Silver Lake neighborhood of L.A.
