Who is Giovanni Pernice Partner? Wife Or Girlfriend Is He Married?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Professional Dancer Giovanni Pernice is back for his seventh year of dancing magic on Strictly. Who is his partner? 

Pernice had first entered the Ballroom Strictly Come dancing in 2015. He even reached the Grand Finals with Coronation Street actress Georgia May Foote. Unfortunately, the duo was not able to win in 2015.

Giovanni started dancing at the age of 14 after he moved to Bologna. He specializes in the Latin style of dancing.

Pernice also holds the Guinness world record for the most jive kicks and flicks in 30 seconds which he achieved on the set of Strictly Come Dancing: It Takes Two.

Giovanni Pernice Wife Or Girlfriend: Is He Married?

Giovanni is not a married man. He does have a partner. In fact, he is currently dating Maura Higgins who was a star of Love Island.

Pernice has previously dated stars like Ashley Roberts, Jess Wright, and Georgia May Foote.

He dated Pussycat Dolls singer Ashley Roberts in 2018 when she had appeared in the Strictly. They dated for a year and broke up in January 2020.

Maura Higgins also had been in a relationship prior to meeting Giovanni. The Love Island star had dated her co-star Chris Taylor. She ended up breaking up with Chris in May this year. The couple went their separate ways as friends.

Meet Giovanni Pernice Family

Giovanni has not made any public information about his family. He is from Sicily but his ethnicity is also unknown.

When it comes to height, the professional dancer Pernice is 5 feet 10 inches tall.

Giovanni Pernice Knows Sign Language

This year in Strictly come dancing, the show welcomed their very first deaf dancer, actress Rose Ayling-Ellis. She made her debut in Saturday night’s launch show (18 September), where it was revealed that she had been paired with Giovanni Pernice.

During the interview, the viewers noticed that the hosts Tess Daly and Claudia Winkleman were communicating with Ayling-Ellis using sign language.

It was also revealed that Pernice had learned sign language for certain words in preparation for the rehearsals during Ayling-Ellis’ first sit-down with Pernice. Viewers praised Pernice for the inclusivity.

What is Giovanni Pernice Net Worth?

The 31-year-old professional dancers’ net worth is estimated to be around $1.5 Million. His net worth is estimated to have been accumulated through his successful dancing career.
