Do They Still Make Kobe Bryant Grinches?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 18, 2024

In recent years, the iconic Nike Kobe "Grinch" sneakers have garnered immense attention and intrigue among sneaker enthusiasts and basketball fans alike. The original "Grinch" colorway, inspired by the classic holiday character, has now seen a resurgence with the release of the Nike Kobe 6 Protro "Reverse Grinch." Let's delve into the details surrounding this highly anticipated sequel to the beloved "Grinch" style.

The Origin of the "Grinch" Sneakers

The "Grinch" sneakers made their debut on December 25th, 2010, during a Christmas-day showdown between the Los Angeles Lakers and Miami Heat. Kobe Bryant, revered as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, donned the original "Grinch" colorway, sparking significant excitement among viewers and establishing it as one of the most iconic Christmas-related sneakers in history.

The Sequel: Nike Kobe 6 Protro "Reverse Grinch"

The Nike Kobe 6 Protro "Reverse Grinch" serves as a sequel to the beloved "Grinch" style. The sneaker's Protro edition covers the majority of the basketball sneaker in a striking "Crimson" flair, while branding hits and laces take on a "Green Apple" hue, giving rise to the "Reverse Grinch" moniker. This highly anticipated release formed part of Nike's end-of-year Basketball collection and was made available in Men and Big Kid's sizing, with prices set at $190 USD and $125 USD, respectively.

Release and Availability

The "Reverse Grinch" was initially available as a Player-Exclusive (PE) until it was confirmed that a retail launch would occur during the Holiday 2023 season. The sneakers were made available at select locations via the Nike SNKRS Pass, with a subsequent release on the Nike SNKRS app and numerous retailers worldwide. Additionally, the sneakers were available at select retailers such as Foot Locker, Feature, Shoe Palace, Xhibition, and Sneakersnstuff.

Increased Production and Demand

Vanessa Bryant, leading the Kobe Bryant Estate, worked closely with Nike to reverse the iconic narrative for the thirteenth anniversary of the original Kobe 6 "Grinch." Notably, she shared that the team at Nike informed her that the sequel to the "Green Mamba" pair from 2010 saw an increase in production by three, indicating a commitment to meeting the heightened demand for this highly sought-after release.

Ongoing Anticipation and Sneaker Culture Impact

The release of the "Reverse Grinch" Kobe 6 Protro sparked widespread anticipation and enthusiasm within the sneaker community. The sneakers, mostly styled in a vibrant crimson hue, have become a symbol of the ongoing legacy of Kobe Bryant's impact on and off the basketball court. The sneakers continue to captivate fans and enthusiasts, representing a fusion of iconic design and modern technology, making them a cherished addition to any collection.

The Nike Kobe 6 Protro "Reverse Grinch" serves as a testament to the enduring legacy of Kobe Bryant's influence on the sneaker culture. Its release represents a significant moment for fans and collectors, offering an opportunity to own a piece of basketball history while honoring the enduring impact of the legendary player. With its bold design, innovative technology, and profound cultural significance, the "Reverse Grinch" Kobe 6 Protro continues to resonate with sneaker enthusiasts and basketball fans worldwide.
