Erykah Badu children: Meet Seven Sirius Benjamin, Puma Sabti Curry, Mars Merkaba Thedford

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Erkyah Badu is an American actress, singer, and record producer. Badu is a mother of three namely; Seven Sirius Benjamin, Puma Sabti Curry and Mars Merkaba Thedford.

Table of Content

Meet Seven Sirius Benjamin, Puma Sabti Curry, Mars Merkaba Thedford

Seven Sirius Benjamin

Seven Sirius Benjamin is a well-known American who is best known as the son of Hip-Hop legends André 3000 and Erykah Badu. Seven Sirius Benjamin was born on November 18, 1997, in Dallas, Texas, USA. Seven Sirius is a very shy individual who avoids public attention. He rose to prominence as a result of his father, André 3000, and mother, Erykah Badu.

Puma Sabti Curry

Erkyah’s second daughter is Puma Sabti Curry. She was born on July 5, 2004 in Dallas, Texas, USA.

Mars Merkaba Thedford

Mars Merkaba Thedford (born February 1, 2009) is Erykah Badu and Jay Electronica’s only child. Though she has half-siblings through her mother, Mars is her parents’ only child, and this is reflected in the love they have lavished on her.
