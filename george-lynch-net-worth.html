What is George Lynch's Net Worth?

George Lynch is an American musician and songwriter who has a net worth of $5 million. Born in Spokane, Washington, George Lynch was raised in Auburn, California. He began pursuing a career as a professional guitarist in the mid-70s, and made his living teaching guitar, and touring with various groups, including The Boyz. He also played with Ozzy Osbourne very briefly. He joined the band, Dokken, in 1980, and the group went on to release a number of successful albums, eventually earning a Grammy nomination in 1990. They disbanded in 1989, and Mr. Lynch started his own band, Lynch Mob, and then shifted his focus to his family.

He returned to the studio in 1993 to release a solo album. In 1994, the original members of Dokken reunited. They went on to release two albums before going their separate ways again. Since then, he has reunited Lynch Mob and also performed as part of the duo Lynch/Pilson. He is currently performing with two members of Dokken, as Tooth & Nail.
