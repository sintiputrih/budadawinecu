NT Rama Rao Jr Personal Information/ Bio

Junior NTR was born in Hyderabad on 20th May 1983. His father’s name is Nandamuri Harikrishna and his mother’s name is Shalini Bhaskar. His father is a former Telugu cinema actor and politician. NT Rama Rao Jr is a grandchild of former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh NT Rama Rao. He completed his schooling at Vidyaranya High School Hyderabad and later he got admission to St. Mary’s College Hyderabad for his further education.

He is married to Lakshmi Pranathi, the daughter of famous businessman Narne Srinivasa Rao. Together they are having two sons Nandamuri Abhay Ram and Nandamuri Bhargav Ram.
