Suleka Mathew Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 6, 2024

Suleka Mathew:

$1.4 million

Suleka Mathew is a Canadian actress. She debuted during the 1980s, first appearing in an episode of 21 Jump Street. In the 1990s, she had small roles in the films Run and The Hitman. Her first notable role on television was as D.A. Cordelia Warren on The Crow: Stairway to Heaven. She gained wider recognition for starring as Dr. Sunita ‘Sunny’ Ramen on the Canadian series Da Vinci’s Inquest. The following decade, she played Dr. Janet Gibson on the sci-fi drama series Stephen King’s Dead Zone. Later, she starred as Sara Jackson in the American series Men in Trees. She also became known for her role as Bobbie Jackson on the TNT series Hawthorne. As of 2017, she stars as Arlene Branch on the TV series Claws.
