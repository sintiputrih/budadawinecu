Say My Name Lyrics

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Say My Name is a popular R&B song recorded by the American girl group, Destiny’s Child.

Released in late 1999 as the lead single from their album “The Writing’s on the Wall.”

The song was a massive commercial success, reaching the top of the charts in several countries, including the United States, United Kingdom, and Australia
