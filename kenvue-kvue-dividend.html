Dividend Capture Strategy

A dividend capture strategy is a method used by investors to maximize their returns from dividend-paying stocks like Kenvue Inc. (KVUE). By understanding the concept of the ex-dividend date, investors can strategically time their stock purchases to receive the upcoming dividend payment.

The ex-dividend date is the crucial date that determines whether an investor is entitled to receive the dividend payment. In the case of Kenvue, if you purchase the stock before the ex-dividend date, you will qualify for the next dividend payment. It’s important to note that the ex-dividend date is typically a few days before the actual payment date, which provides sufficient time for the stock purchase to settle.

To illustrate, suppose the ex-dividend date for Kenvue Inc. is February 13, 2024, and the dividend payment is scheduled for February 28, 2024. As an investor, to capture the dividend, you would need to buy shares of KVUE before February 13, 2024.

Once you’ve successfully acquired the shares before the ex-dividend date, you become eligible to receive the upcoming dividend payment. Afterward, you can choose to hold the shares for the long term or sell them once the stock price recovers, ideally capturing both the dividend and any potential capital appreciation. However, it’s essential to consider factors such as transaction costs and tax implications when executing a dividend capture strategy.

Remember, the dividend capture strategy requires careful timing and thorough research. It’s crucial to monitor the ex-dividend dates and dividend payment schedules for stocks such as Kenvue Inc. to optimize your dividend income.
