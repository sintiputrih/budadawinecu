Is McGregor a billionaire?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 22, 2024

Conor Anthony McGregor is a professional mixed martial artist from Ireland.
He was a former featherweight and lightweight double champion in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC).

He is the first UFC fighter to simultaneously hold titles in two weight divisions.
He was formerly the lightweight and featherweight champion of Cage Warriors.

Conor Anthony McGregor was born in Crumlin, Dublin. His parents are Tony and Margaret McGregor.
He was raised in Crumlin and educated there, first at the Gaelscoil Scoil Mológa in Harold’s Cross and then at the Gaelcholáiste Coláiste in Tallaght, where he also discovered his love for football and his enthusiasm for the game.

Is McGregor a billionaire?

No, the UFC champion fighter is not a billionaire but rather a millionaire. He is estimated to be worth $200 million. That net worth includes the roughly $100 million payday Conor earned from his August 2017 fight against Floyd Mayweather, the $50 million from his October 2018 fight against Khabib, and an estimated $200 million pre-tax earnings from the April 2021 sale of his whiskey brand Proper No. Twelve.

At the age of 18, McGregor made his mixed martial arts debut against Kieran Campbell in a pro match for the Irish Ring of Truth promotion in Dublin on February 17, 2007.
First-round technical knockout (TKO) gave him the victory.

He became professional after the fight and was hired by the Irish Cage of Truth promotion.
In 2008, McGregor started working out with John Kavanagh at Dublin’s Straight Blast Gym (SBG).
