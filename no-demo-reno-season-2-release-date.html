No Demo Reno Rating & Reviews

The show’s first season, which finished in May, drew over 25.1 million viewers and increased HGTV’s 8 p.m. to 9 p.m. time slot ratings by 36%. The ten one-hour episodes, each featuring two homes, aired as a block on Saturday mornings and must have met the audience requirements for a second season on HGTV. The network announced on Monday that there will be ten additional episodes.

In June, HGTV president Jane Latman praised the show and hinted that No Demo Reno would return. Her popularity is evidenced by the fast response on social media. Todryk announced her news late Sunday, and her followers responded with 80,000 likes and 3,800 heartfelt comments.

Todryk’s Instagram account, @theramblingredhead, has over 1 million followers, and she uses it to share every day and comically realistic thoughts on motherhood, home design, fashion, and beauty.
