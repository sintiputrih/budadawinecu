Eric Decker Net Worth: How the Former NFL Star Built His Fortune

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Eric Decker is a former American football wide receiver who played in the National Football League (NFL) for eight seasons. He played college football and college baseball at the University of Minnesota, and was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the third round of the 2010 NFL Draft. After four seasons with the Broncos, Decker played for the New York Jets for three years, then the Tennessee Titans for one season. He retired from football in 2018, but he has not stopped making money. Here is how Eric Decker built his net worth and what he is doing now.

Eric Decker’s NFL Career and Earnings

Eric Decker had a successful career in the NFL, playing as a wide receiver for three different teams. He was known for his reliable hands, route-running skills, and red-zone efficiency. He had several standout seasons, especially in 2012 and 2013, when he was part of the record-breaking Denver Broncos offense led by Peyton Manning. He reached the Super Bowl XLVIII with the Broncos in 2013, but they lost to the Seattle Seahawks.

According to Spotrac, Eric Decker earned a total of $36.3 million from his NFL contracts. His highest salary was $7.25 million in 2016, when he was with the New York Jets. His lowest salary was $320,000 in 2010, when he was a rookie with the Denver Broncos. His average annual salary was $4.5 million.

Eric Decker’s Endorsements and Business Ventures

Besides his NFL earnings, Eric Decker also made money from various endorsements and business ventures. He has partnered with brands such as Buffalo David Bitton, Old Spice, Remington, Rawlings, and Wrangler. He also launched his own clothing line called Deckers Dogs, which supports service dogs for veterans.

Eric Decker is also a reality TV star, along with his wife Jessie James Decker, who is a country singer and entrepreneur. The couple starred in their own show called Eric & Jessie: Game On, which aired for three seasons on E! from 2013 to 2017. The show followed their personal and professional lives as they balanced their careers and family.

Eric Decker is also involved in real estate, as he and his wife have bought and sold several properties over the years. They currently live in Nashville, Tennessee, where they own a mansion worth $4.5 million.

Eric Decker’s Net Worth in 2023

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Eric Decker has a net worth of $10 million as of 2023. This is based on his NFL earnings, endorsements, business ventures, and investments. He also shares his wealth with his wife Jessie James Decker, who has a net worth of $10 million as well.

Eric Decker is enjoying his retirement from football and spending more time with his family. He and his wife have four children: daughter Vivianne Rose, son Eric Thomas II, son Forrest Bradley, and daughter Brooklyn Rae. They also have two dogs named Jake and Jenny.

Eric Decker is still active on social media, where he posts updates on his life and projects. He has over 1.2 million followers on Instagram and over 1 million followers on Twitter. He also supports various charitable causes, such as Deckers Dogs Foundation, St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, and United Way.

Eric Decker is an example of a former NFL star who has successfully transitioned to other fields after his playing career. He has built a fortune from his diverse sources of income and has maintained a happy and healthy lifestyle with his family.
