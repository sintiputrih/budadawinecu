JB Smoove Family Life

JB Smoove parents are Floyd and Elizabeth Brooks. He was born in Plymouth, North Carolina on December 16, 1965.

He was raised by his parent and moved from North Carolina to Mount Vernon, New York. He and his family lived in the Levister Towers housing project.

The comedian also has two younger brothers with whom he shares a close bond and grew up in Mount Vernon in the same house.

Smoove spent adequate time visiting his mother and his extended maternal family in the Plymount area, which shaped his comedic career and style.

Sadly, his father passed away from diabetes when he was just a 15-year-old kid. It was a difficult time for him and his family.

The 58 years old actor completed his high school education at Mount Vernon High School and later went to Norfolk State University in Virginia.

During the early year of his life, he worked as a perfume formulator and also used to sell fire extinguishers door-to-door.

It was when he started doing stand-up comedy he decided to keep one stage name and from Jerry Crooks to J.B. and Kept Smoove as his last name.

Since 2018, he has been following a vegan lifestyle and committed to a healthy and better lifestyle.

In 2007, JB Smoove got married to singer Shahidah Omar and is now living in Los Angeles, USA.
