Shiloh Jolie Pitt's 2021 look at Marvel Eternals red carpet stuns fans

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

On Monday, October 18, 2021, Angelina Jolie made a rare appearance with her children on the Eternals red carpet and fans were struck by how breathtaking the actress’ daughter, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt, looked.

Unlike many other celebs, Angelina does not shy away from bringing her kids in front of the cameras once in a while. The mother of six is often spotted out and about with her children. But, red carpet appearances have still been a rarity for the lot.

Shiloh Jolie Pitt looks stunning in 2021

Shiloh, who is 15 now, stepped out in a stunning dress inspired by Angelina’s 1998 Emmy Awards’ beige dress.

The Eternals star is known to wear the colour beige quite often but Shiloh certainly pulled it off in her own way. The teen’s stunning dress had a wide slit on the right leg, adding more chicness to the look.

Shiloh’s look also featured a pair of white shoes, light and minimalistic makeup and a pair of golden anklets.

While the star kid looked gorgeous, many fans were stunned by how different she looks now compared to her earlier pictures.

Shiloh, who is Angelina and Brad’s first biological kid, has often been eyed by the former couple’s fans. Many have often been struck by Shiloh’s fashion sense, who often wore more androgynous clothes as a young kid.

The star kid also preferred to keep her hair short as a youngling and wore suits on her red carpet appearance with her mum and dad.

Angelina told to Vanity Fair in a 2010 interview that her daughter has been exploring gender since age three. “She wants to be a boy, so we had to cut her hair. She likes to wear boy clothes,” the actress said.

Over the years, the star kid was always encouraged by her parents to dress the way she desired. Often times as a child, we have seen her don casuals like polo shirts, jackets, shorts, jeans and even oversized tracksuits.

However, Shiloh’s sense of self and style has evolved a lot in 2021. She broke the internet at the beginning of the year after being photographed in denim cut-offs, and a black hoodie.

She also had pierced ears and had grown her hair long, with her locks tied up in a bun.

Angelina Jolie and children walk the ‘Eternals’ red carpet

Angelina, who is undergoing a contentious divorce with Brad Pitt, stepped out in a stunning, strapless brown gown with a gold lip piece during the Eternals red carpet.

The actress brought five of her kids -Maddox Jolie-Pitt, Vivienne Jolie-Pitt, Knox Jolie-Pitt, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt, and Zahara Jolie-Pitt – to the event.

Eternals has been one of Marvel’s most-anticipated releases this year. The film stars major celebrities including Richard Madden, Salma Hayek, Kumail Nanjiani, Kit Harington, Gemma Chan and more.

It has been directed by Academy Award-winning director, Chloe Zhao, and is set to introduce a new set of superheroes, the immortals, to help fans delve deeper into marvel’s history.
