Claudia Bachteler Obituary, Death – It is with heavy hearts that we announce the passing of Claudia Bachteler, a resident of Riverside Drive, New York. Claudia, 36, tragically took her own life on October 10, 2023, leaving her friends and family in profound grief. Claudia was a woman of immense talent and passion.

Born on June 14, 1987, she grew up in Riverside Drive and attended local schools, where she demonstrated a natural aptitude for the arts. She pursued her love for painting and became a renowned artist in her own right, showcasing her work in numerous galleries across the city. Claudia’s vibrant and expressive canvases were a reflection of her inner world, which was filled with creativity, depth, and sensitivity.

Her untimely passing reminds us of the importance of addressing mental health and supporting those who may be silently struggling. Claudia leaves behind a loving family, including her parents, David and Lily, and a tight-knit group of friends who remember her as a warm and compassionate soul. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to be made to local mental health organizations, in the hope of bringing greater awareness to the issues Claudia faced. Claudia’s memory will forever live on through her art and in the hearts of those who knew and loved her. May she find the peace she sought in her journey beyond this world.
