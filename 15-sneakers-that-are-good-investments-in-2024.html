15 Sneakers that are Good Investments in 2024

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 3, 2024

Investing in sneakers has become a popular trend, with many enthusiasts seeking to make profitable purchases. When considering sneakers as an investment, it's crucial to identify the most sought-after models that are likely to retain or increase in value over time. In this comprehensive article, we will delve into 15 sneakers that are considered good investments in 2024. Each sneaker will be thoroughly analyzed to provide detailed information for potential investors.

Nike Air Jordan 1 Retro High "Off-White Chicago"

The Nike Air Jordan 1 Retro High "Off-White Chicago" stands as a highly coveted sneaker due to its collaboration with the fashion label Off-White. This sneaker's limited release and its iconic Chicago colorway contribute to its high desirability among sneaker collectors and investors alike.

Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 "Zebra"

The Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 "Zebra" has consistently proven to be a strong investment due to its recognizable design and association with Kanye West. Its distinct aesthetic, combined with limited releases, has led to high resale values and sustained demand.

Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Bred Toe"

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Bred Toe" is a timeless classic that continues to be a valuable investment. Its heritage, iconic color blocking, and strong cultural significance within sneaker culture make it a sought-after model for both collectors and investors.

Nike Dunk Low Pro SB "Paris"

The Nike Dunk Low Pro SB "Paris" is a highly sought-after sneaker, particularly due to its limited production and exclusive distribution. Its unique color scheme and association with skateboarding culture contribute to its status as a desirable investment piece.

Adidas Human Race NMD Pharrell x Chanel

The collaboration between Pharrell Williams, Adidas, and the luxury brand Chanel has resulted in the creation of the Adidas Human Race NMD Pharrell x Chanel. This exclusive sneaker represents a fusion of high fashion and streetwear, making it a valuable investment piece for those seeking to diversify their sneaker portfolios.

Air Jordan 4 Retro Eminem Encore

The Air Jordan 4 Retro Eminem Encore is a rare and highly sought-after sneaker, primarily due to its limited production and association with the iconic rapper Eminem. Its exclusivity and cultural significance make it a compelling investment choice for sneaker enthusiasts.

Nike Dunk High Pro SB "FLOM"

The Nike Dunk High Pro SB "FLOM" (For Love or Money) is a rare and sought-after sneaker known for its distinctive design and limited availability. Its connection to sneaker customizer Futura and its scarcity in the market contribute to its status as a valuable investment piece.

Adidas NMD Hu Pharrell x Billionaire Boys Club "Cotton Candy"

The collaboration between Pharrell Williams, Adidas, and Billionaire Boys Club has resulted in the creation of the Adidas NMD Hu Pharrell x Billionaire Boys Club "Cotton Candy." This vibrant and exclusive sneaker represents a convergence of art, fashion, and street culture, making it an attractive investment option.

Nike Air Max 1/97 Sean Wotherspoon

The Nike Air Max 1/97 Sean Wotherspoon is celebrated for its unique design and the story behind its creation. Its limited release and distinctive aesthetic have contributed to its status as a valuable investment piece within the sneaker market.

Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Shattered Backboard"

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Shattered Backboard" pays homage to a significant moment in Michael Jordan's career and features a distinctive colorway. Its cultural significance and limited releases have solidified its position as a desirable investment sneaker.

Adidas Yeezy Boost 700 "Wave Runner"

The Adidas Yeezy Boost 700 "Wave Runner" is a standout model within the Yeezy lineup, known for its unique design and robust demand. Its limited availability and Kanye West's influence have contributed to its status as a strong investment option.

Nike SB Dunk Low "Paris"

The Nike SB Dunk Low "Paris" is a rare and highly sought-after sneaker, recognized for its collaboration with French artist Bernard Buffet. Its artistic connection and scarcity in the market make it an appealing investment choice for sneaker enthusiasts.

Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Royal"

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG "Royal" is a timeless classic within the Jordan Brand lineup, celebrated for its iconic colorway and historical significance. Its enduring appeal and cultural resonance position it as a valuable investment within the sneaker market.

Adidas Yeezy Boost 750 "Glow in the Dark"

The Adidas Yeezy Boost 750 "Glow in the Dark" stands out for its unique luminous feature and association with Kanye West's Yeezy brand. Its limited production and distinctive design make it an intriguing investment option for sneaker collectors.

Nike Air Max 90 "Bacon"

The Nike Air Max 90 "Bacon" is a highly coveted sneaker recognized for its collaboration with the renowned sneaker boutique, Dave's Quality Meat (DQM). Its limited release and distinctive color scheme have solidified its status as a valuable investment piece.

Investing in sneakers requires careful consideration and research, especially when aiming to identify models with strong investment potential. The sneakers highlighted in this article represent a diverse range of styles, collaborations, and cultural significance, making them compelling choices for individuals seeking to build a valuable sneaker collection or investment portfolio. As with any investment, thorough due diligence and an understanding of market trends are essential when considering the purchase of sneakers for investment purposes.
