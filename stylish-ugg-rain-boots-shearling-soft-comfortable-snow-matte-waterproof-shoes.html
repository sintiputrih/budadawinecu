Its genuine shearling lining offers us exactly what we’d expect of an Ugg boot, which is warmth, comfort and happiness — three things we thought the sun stole with it when it decided to hide away for the day. One reason these rain boots have so many five star reviews is because they’re actually fashionable. No, really! Instead of the traditional super-shiny, squeaky finish we expect from other rubber boots, these Shelby boots have a trendy matte finish. Don’t worry though, because they are still waterproof and a safe bet for strutting through puddles in.
