Dua Lipas Miu Miu Look Includes a White Bra and Brown Skirt Set

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Dua Lipa showed off a high-fashion take on the exposed-lingerie trend this week.

In her latest Instagram photo dump, the pop star shared pics of a head-to-toe Miu Miu look she wore recently. It included a long brown skirt with a utilitywear-inspired belt and a gold buckle with the brand’s logo, plus a matching bomber jacket. Lipa didn’t wear a top with the set, but instead opted for a dainty white ribbed balconette bra by the fashion house, with the logo stamped in black in the middle.

The “Sweetest Pie” singer accessorized with heaps of gold jewelry, including layers of chunky necklaces and a gold watch. She also wore brown rectangle glasses and carried a brown distressed-leather bag.

“Call me beep me if u wanna reach me,” Lipa wrote in the post caption.

The stylish singer’s latest fashion-filled photo dump comes after she shared a string of snaps of her recent fiery Versace looks. Lipa co-designed a collection with Donatella Versace herself for the fashion house called “La Vacanza” and modeled two of their new bikinis on Instagram. Earlier this week, she posed atop a cotton-candy pink convertible in a sparkling pink two-piece swimsuit. Glittering pink sequins covered the set, which also featured gold chain straps that wrapped around her torso and waist. She kept the monochromatic pink theme going with a matching sparkling mini handbag and a pair of metallic pink platform-heeled sandals.
