Peter Pan Live!: Best Tweets on Allison Williams, Christopher Walken

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 12, 2024

That Nana though! Whether it was to hate-watch or to be entertained, millions of people tuned in to NBC’s musical production Peter Pan Live! on Thursday, Dec. 4. And before saying goodbye to Tinkerbell and the Lost Boys, they shared their reviews on social media.

Us Weekly has rounded up some of the most clever and hilarious Twitter reactions to the three-hour television event. What we’ve learned?
