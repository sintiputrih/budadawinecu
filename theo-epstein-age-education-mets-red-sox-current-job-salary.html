Theo Epstein Age, Education, Mets, Red Sox, Current Job, Salary

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 21, 2024

Theo Nathaniel Epstein is an American Major League Baseball executive.

He previously worked as the president of baseball operations for the Chicago Cubs.

Theo Epstein Age

How old is Theo Epstein? Theo Epstein is 4 years old. He was born on December 29, 1973.

Theo Epstein Education

Which college did Theo Epstein attend? Theo Epstein went to Yale University and lived at Jonathan Edwards College. He was the Yale Daily News’s sports editor.

Theo Epstein Mets

Did Theo Epstein work for the Mets? No, he did not. Throughout his career he has worked for teams including San Diego Padres, Boston Red Sox and Chicago Cubs

Theo Epstein Red Sox

Did Theo Epstein work for the Boston Red Sox? Yes, he did. He was the vice president and general manager for the Boston Red Sox  He worked for the team for nine seasons

Theo Epstein Current Job

What is Theo Epstein doing now? Theo Epstein currently works for MLB as a consultant.

MLB hired Epstein as a consultant for “on-field matters” in January 2021. Later that year, he joined Arctos Sports Partners, a private equity group.

Theo Epstein Salary

How much does Theo Epstein earn? Theo Epstein’s current salary is unknown. However, at his peak he earned a salary of $10 million per year.
