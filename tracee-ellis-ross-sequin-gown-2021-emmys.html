Tracee Ellis Ross in Sexy, Sequined Valentino at 2021 Emmy Awards

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Tracee Ellis Ross had a standout style moment on the 2021 Emmy Award's red carpet.

The Black-ish actress arrived at the 73rd annual Primetime Emmy Awards in a vibrant red sequined, one-shouldered gown by Valentino Haute Couture, paired with diamond-encrusted bangles, bold rings courtesy of Tiffany & Co., and coordinating pointed-toe pumps. Ross opted for a sultry approach to her glam for the evening, wearing her natural, voluminous curls and opting for a cocoa-hued lip for her makeup.

Ross's latest red-carpet appearance comes after her stunning entrance at the 2021 Met Gala last week, when she rocked a royal-blue ensemble courtesy of Balenciaga Couture paired with an angular bob.

Rich Fury//Getty Images

Ross shared a series of throwback images from Emmy Awards past on Instagram ahead of her arrival to this year's ceremony.

"EMMY PHOTO DUMP," Ross captioned the post.

Ross recently spoke to BAZAAR.com about her excitement surrounding the return to red carpets after a year away from in-person events due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

"[Sharing my outfits] is a way to put joy in action for me. When I get dressed, I think to myself, 'Ah, I like her, I remember her!' She got lost a little bit," said Ross. "During the pandemic, I continued working and there was no question that I continued putting on my lipstick, putting on a beautiful top and earrings and necklaces and things like that, but to be going back out into the world and getting dressed for thatâ€”I missed that part of my life."
