Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste is a 37 years old fashion designer and a cast on Project Runway. Learn About his Wikipedia, Parents And Instagram details below.

Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste is a fashion designer and a contestant of Project Runaway premiering on BravoTV on October 14.

Project runaway is an American reality television series focusing on fashion design. The contestants compete to create the best clothes and have time, materials, and theme restrictions.

Project Runway: Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste Age

Fashion designer Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste’s age is 37 years old.

Prajjé grew up in Haiti, where his grandmother raised him.

At the age of 14, he moved to the United States, when he realized his love of fashion could flourish into a profession, which was encouraged by his adoptive parents.

The fashion designer graduated from the prestigious Massachusetts College of Art in Boston and later launched his self-titled line, Prajjé Oscar.

Prajjé now resides in Philadelphia, looking for what is next in his career and personal life. And like many others during these challenging times, he pivoted his work to a leisurewear line and moved his business online.

Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste Wikipedia Explored

Prajjé Oscar Jean-Baptiste is yet to feature on the official page of Wikipedia.

Baptiste is a Haitian-born, Philadelphia-based fashion designer known for his exquisite evening gowns.

His eponymous fashion brand has made it in some prestigious publications and TV programs, including ELLE Magazine, Arise Magazine, The Boston Globe, the Boston Herald, and Good Morning America.
