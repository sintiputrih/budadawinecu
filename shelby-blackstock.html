There were undoubtedly obstacles for Shelby. But, he has put together a team that includes a head mechanic and a pit crew.

Quick Facts

Early Life and Childhood

Shelby Blackstock was born Shelby Steven McEntire Blackstock on February 23rd, 1990. Shelby was born and raised in Nashville, Tennessee, USA.

He is of American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity.

Unlike most children, Shelby showed interest in automobiles, but his obsession was different. Shelby was a brilliant boy good at studies and was a loyal son to his parents. He was very much into the 90s race car scene and followed them like his religion.

Growing up, Shelby always looked up to Michael Schumacher. Michael competed in F1 races. He held many records which are still unbroken to this day.

Shelby was determined from childhood and wanted to become like his heroes. He wanted a career in race car driving.

With the history of Mustang and its image, it had built a win over Ferrari in 1969. Every kid dreamt of riding a Mustang, and so did Shelby. But, Shelby successfully achieved his dream and followed his passion.

Shelby got his primary education from a local school in his hometown, Nashville.

Later, he joined Bob Bondurant’s race school. It is an American performance driving school that specializes in many racing courses. It was an excellent opportunity for Shelby to enhance his knowledge of racing.

However, he dropped out to pursue a full-time career in racing.

Shelby Blackstock family

Shelby Blackstock’s mother is Reba McEntire, and his father is Narvel Blackstock.

Talking about Shelby’s parents, were always supportive of his decisions. They loved him and supported the choices that helped him become a race car driver.

Shelby has two half-sisters named Shawna and Chassidy. He also has an older half-brother named Brandon Blackstock. Although half brothers and sisters, they share love and respect for each other.

Reba McEntire (Mother)

Shelby’s mother, Reba Nell McEntire, is an American country singer and actress. She began her career by singing in her high school band named Kiowa High School band.

Reba is a famous singer in the world of country music who contributed a lot to that genre. In university, she sang the national anthem at the National Finals Rodeo in Oklahoma. Her performance caught the eye of country artist Red Steagall.

Red was the one who brought her to Nashville and gave her a ladder to climb the success stage. It was the breakthrough part of her career. A year later, Reba signed a contract with Mercury Records, and the rest is history.

Today, Reba is one of the most prominent figures in the music world with various hits. She has also contributed as an actress in many movies, broadway, and television series.

Narvel was previously married to Elisa Gayle Ritter (1973-1988), with whom he had three children. His marriage with Reba McEntire lasted from (1989- 2015).

Shelby Blackstock relationship status

Shelby is married with his longtime girlfriend, Marissa Branch. The couple celebrated their 6th anniversary on May 15th, 2022. After dating for six years they got married on May 4.

The couple seems very happy and often shares photos of them on Social Media. Shelby shares adorable pictures with his wife on his social media account. The lovers have two dogs together named Watson and Belle.

Career

Shelby started his career in racing after he dropped out of Bob Bondurant’s race school. His first significant appearance in a tournament was in the Skip Barber competition.

His professional debut as a driver started in 2011 when he signed a contract with Ford Mustang. He drove a Ford Mustang in the Continental Tire SportsCar Challenge.

The next competition he appeared in was in the same year. Shelby participated in the Skip Barber National Championship, where he finished fifth. At the time, he was getting noticed for his professional driving skills.

Shelby had a determination to make a name for himself in the American racing scene.

Shelby participated in USF2000 National Championship with Andretti Autosport. Andretti Autosport is an auto racing team. Shelby finished eighth in points along with the best finish of fourth at Road America.

Later in 2013, he moved up with the same auto racing team to the Pro Mazda Championship. Finishing third in points, he had indeed caught the media’s eye. Along with this, he achieved six podium finishes in 16 races.

Shelby was at the pinnacle of his success, and people were getting more interested in him. In 2014 he continued with Pro Mazda but fell to fourth in points. He still managed to bag six podium finishes but no race victory.

In 2015 Blackstock signed to race for Andretti Autosport in the Indy Lights series. Considering his achievements, he is a successful race car driver from Nashville. Shelby has been able to accumulate a big fan following over the years.
