Career

Andrew currently serves as the Chief Meteorologist at Weather Chaser Consulting and holds the position of Technology and Merchandising Pro at Apple. He started his career as a Meteorology Intern at CBS News in June 2012, assisting the chief meteorologist in creating daily forecasts and graphics for television.

From November 2015 to November 2019, he worked as a meteorologist at RNN-TV. Andrew joined Apple in November 2013, initially as a Specialist and later as a Creative Pro, focusing on photography and videography.

Since June 2023, he has been serving as a Technology and Merchandising Pro at Apple, managing demo and internal technology, merchandising the store, and educating the team on current and new technology for better customer support.
