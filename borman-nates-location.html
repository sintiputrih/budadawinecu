Borderlands 3 Borman Nates Location

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 18, 2024

Borman Nates is a miniboss in BL3 that you can beat over and over again to get some Legendary loot, such as the Psycho Stabber pistol. The only potential issue is where to find the location of Borman Nates. If you haven’t explored the game too thoroughly, you might have missed him completely. The loot he can drop, however, is well-worth the effort to find him. We’re here to help you out on this journey with our Borderlands 3 Borman Nates Location guide.

Where to Find Borman Nates in Borderlands 3?

To find the location of Borman Nates in Borderlands 3, you have to go to Meridian Outskirts on Promethea. Go to the large, central area, the one that’s kinda shaped like a pair of wings. In the west of the area, there’s a large structure that you can climb onto. This is the place where you complete the Maliwannabees quest in the main campaign, by the way. Just explore the structure (which seems to be the remains of a bombed-out highway or something), and you’ll find Borman walking around. When you dispatch Borman, there’s a chance of about 10% he’ll drop a Legendary weapon. On the list of possible Legendary gear are the Cutsman, the Sawbar, and the Psycho Stabber. Get it? Borman Nates? Norman Bates? Hitchcock’s “Psycho”? I hate the humor in this game. Anyways, if you get super-lucky, he might even drop two Legendaries. Out of the three, there’s further 10% chance it’ll be the Psycho Stabber. The dedicated drops are not affected by modifiers. So, good luck with your farming.
