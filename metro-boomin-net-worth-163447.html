Metro Boomin is a record producer from St. Louis, MO, born Leland Wayne on September 16, 1993. He has produced instrumentals for many popular hip hop artists, such as Ludacris, Migos, Future, Wiz Khalifa, and Nicki Minaj. His work has been highly acclaimed and has helped to shape the sound of modern hip hop.
