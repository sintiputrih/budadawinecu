Where is Gary Kemp now? What is Gary Kemp doing now?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 27, 2024

Gary James Kemp, the renowned English songwriter, musician, and actor, has left an indelible mark on the music scene, particularly as the lead guitarist and principal songwriter for the iconic new wave band Spandau Ballet. In this article, we delve into where Gary Kemp is now and what he is currently passionate about.

Residence and Family Life:

Gary Kemp calls London home, sharing his life with his wife, Lauren Kemp, a talented costume designer, and their three sons named Milo Wolf, Kit, and Rex. Additionally, Gary has a fourth son, Finlay, from his first marriage to Sadie Frost. This familial backdrop provides a glimpse into the multifaceted life Gary leads beyond the spotlight.

Interests and Hobbies:

Beyond the stage and studio, Gary Kemp leads an active lifestyle that reflects his diverse interests. A dedicated cyclist and mountain walker, he finds joy in the outdoors, seeking adventure and solace in nature. His passion extends to collecting the furniture of architect-designer Edward William Godwin, showcasing an appreciation for art and design.

Musical Collaborations:

While Gary Kemp remains a prominent figure in the music industry, his recent endeavors include collaborations that showcase his versatility. As a guitarist and vocalist, he has joined forces with Pink Floyd’s Nick Mason in the psychedelic rock group, Saucerful of Secrets. Nick Mason invited Gary to contribute his musical prowess, aiming to capture the spirit of Pink Floyd’s early music.

Solo Artistry:

Gary Kemp’s solo career continues to thrive, demonstrating his commitment to musical exploration and expression. Beyond the collaborative projects, he remains a prolific solo artist, allowing his creativity to evolve and resonate with a diverse audience.

In the current chapter of Gary Kemp’s life, we find a harmonious blend of family, personal interests, and an enduring passion for music. From the bustling streets of London to the tranquil landscapes explored on cycling and walking excursions, Gary Kemp’s journey is as multifaceted as the melodies he has crafted over the years. As he continues to contribute to the musical landscape, fans can anticipate the ongoing evolution of a true artist.
