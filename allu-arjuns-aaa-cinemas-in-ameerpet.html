Allu Arjuns AAA Cinemas In Ameerpet

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Seems like Tollywood heroes are following a trend of having their own multiplexes. Superstar Mahesh Babu was the first who stepped into this business a few years back with AMB Cinemas and recently Rowdy Star Vijay Deverakonda too made in roads. His multiplex AVD Cinemas is getting ready in his home town Mahbubnagar.

Now Mahesh and Vijay have a company and he is Stylish Star Allu Arjun. In collaboration with leading multiplex chain Asian Cinemas, Allu Arjun is floating AAA Cinemas – Asian Allu Arjun Cinemas.

This multiplex is under construction and the location is the prime spot of twin cities. AAA Cinemas is located in Ameerpet and a picture of AAA Cinema’s logo with Bunny’s photo is doing rounds on social media. It might take another couple of months or probably a year for its completion.

Like Bunny, AAA Cinemas is expected to be more stylish catering urban audiences with fully loaded logistics.

On the work front Bunny is busy with ‘Pushpa’ and recently the villain of the film was confirmed. It will be Malayalam star actor Fahad Faasil who will be seen opposite Bunny.
