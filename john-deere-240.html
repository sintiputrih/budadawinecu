John Deere 240 Garden Tractor is equipped with a Kawasaki FC420V gasoline 1-cylinder air-cooled vertical-shaft, 422cc engine, which delivers 14 hp (10.4 kW) power at rated rpm 3350.

John Deere 240 Garden Tractor featured a 14HP Kawasaki 422cc 1-cyl gasoline engine, 4×2 2WD, 590 to 680 pounds weight, 6 forward and 1 reverse gear, and 3.4 Qts (3.2 L) gears.

John Deere 240 Garden tractor attachments include a 38″ mid-mount mower deck, 46″ mid-mount mower deck, 48″ mid-mount mower deck, snowblower, and blade.
