52 Years Old Murder Suspect Anthony Gray Stockton CA Arrested

Anthony Gray is a 52-year-old man from Stockton, California. He is the primary murder suspect in the Stockton high school stabbing.

Gray has been arrested and held in the San Joaquin County jail. He is charged with murder. Authorities are still looking into the reason for the attack and conducting additional research into the matter.

Only a few details regarding Grey have got released to the media and the general public.

According to reports, Gray reportedly went to the school's front parking lot, parked his car, and then walked over to the victim.

He then repeatedly stabbed the victim before being apprehended by a school district police officer.
