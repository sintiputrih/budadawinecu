Here Is How Much Khanyi Mbaus New Rich Zimbabwean Boyfriend Is Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 27, 2024

TV reality star Khanyi Mbau recently took to her Instagram account to share photos of her Zimbabwean boyfriend. In light of that, we take a look at the young rich guy who stole the star’s heart.

The media personality has found love in the arms of Zimbabwean boyfriend Kudzai Terrence Mushonga popularly known as “Ndege boy” which means airplane boy. The 28-year-old is reported to be a fugitive in his native country following allegations of fraudulent activities against him.

After years of hiding from the Zimbabwean authorities Kudzai reappears on Khanyi’s social media account as her new bae. He is the youngest African to own an airline and is believed to have gained his wealth by defrauding a Zimbabwean Microfinance company.

Kudzai Terrence is a millionaire with a business interest in Gas, Petroleum, and Real Estates. Kudzai “Ndege boy” Terrence owns a fleet of top range cars, Ferraris, Bentleys, Rolls Royce, and latest Range Rovers, and is worth $5.4 million.
