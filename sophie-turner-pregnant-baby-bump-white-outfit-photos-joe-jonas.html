Sophie Turner Shows Off Baby Bump in White Outfit with Joe Jonas

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 4, 2024

Sophie Turner and husband Joe Jonas stepped out for a stroll in Beverly Hills this week, and the actress's growing baby bump was on full display.

The duo sported stylish-casual outfits during their walk, with Turner wearing a white button-down shirt over a tank, matching loose sweatpants, and sneakers. Her bump was visible under her top, and she wore her hair in two braids.

Jonas, meanwhile, wore a tan jacket-and-pants set and silver heeled boots. They both accessorized with sunglasses: Turner a sporty silver pair and Jonas a green pair with orange lenses.

BACKGRID

The Game of Thrones star and the Jonas Brothers singer are expecting their second child together. The pair married in May 2019 and welcomed their first child, daughter Willa, in July 2020.

Ahead of the premiere of her new HBO Max series, The Staircase, Turner opened up to The Wrap about how becoming a mom has changed her. "The concept of family has made me an entirely much more emotional person. The empathy is heightened; the feelings about family are just like overflowing," she said. "It's made me a better actor for becoming, you know, becoming a mother."

Turner and Jonas attended the Met Gala together earlier this month and shared a sweet moment where Jonas embraced his wife's baby bump.
