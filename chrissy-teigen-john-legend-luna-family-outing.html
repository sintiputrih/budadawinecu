Chrissy Teigen and John Legend's Stylish Family Outing With Luna Is Too Cute To Handle

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, January 12, 2024

Less than two weeks since welcoming the newest addition to their family, baby Miles Theodore Stephens, Chrissy Teigen and John Legend stepped out for a stylish outing with 2-year-old daughter Luna yesterday.

Hitting Beverly Hills (possibly for some spaghetti, according to the sign behind them), the trio looked incredibly chic for some quality family bonding. Perfectly mastering the art of post-maternity style, Chrissy Teigen wore a floor-length striped satin jacket, oversize hoop earrings, and a braided ponytail. Complementing her look, John Legend went for an all-black ensemble in a satin bomber jacket.

BACKGRID

But the real style star of the family here is so obviously Luna Stephens (sorry, Chrissy and John). The toddler wore an orange polka dot sundress, denim jacket, and white sneakers for her day out with mom and dad. To accessorize, she wore the cutest white bows in her adorable double bun hairstyle and carried a Gucci handbag on one arm and a leopard stuffed animal in the other (a high-low balance is key, of course). Take note, street style stars.

BACKGRID

If heralding a 2-year-old as my new fashion muse is wrong, I don't want to be right. This is my new look for Summer 2018, thank you Luna.

Teigen also took to her Instagram to share another photo of Luna's outfit—and she's got her posing down pat too.

While newborn Miles obviously didn't join his family for a day out just yet, we did get a sneak-peek at Luna helping out with the baby at home. In a sweet photo posted by Teigen, the new big sister offers her baby brother his pacifier as he takes a nap.

Cutest. Family. Ever.
