Watchmen Actress, Hong Chau Is Still Marked As Unmarried; Achieved Numerous Of Nomination From Downs

The talented personality Hong Chau is one of the known actresses of Hollywood. She is best known for her outstanding performance in the US film Downsizing released in 2017 as a Vietnamese amputee and political activist. On that note, she was nominated for several awards for best-supporting actress. 

The Asian-American ethical actress was born in 1979 and has been active in the movies field since 2006. Currently, she is appearing in superhero drama television series, Watchmen as Lady Trieu where Frances Fisher played as Jane Crawford. Artists like Don Johnson, Jeremy Irons also starred in the Watchmen.

Formative Living

Regarding Chau's early life, she was born in a refugee camp in Thailand. Her parents were among the Vietnamese boat people who run away to Vietnam in 1979. She attended public school and was dependent on Pell Grants.

Later, she attended Boston University, where she learned creative writing. After that, she moved to New York City and studied acting and started to appear in film and television. 

Within a break in the film, there was no looking back for her, and here she is now one of the renowned actresses in Hollywood who has appeared in numerous TV series and movies.

Awards and Nominations

Millions of her fans have appreciated chau's acting. For her role in Downsizing, she was nominated for several awards.
