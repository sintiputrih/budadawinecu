Savannah DeLullo - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 2, 2024

TikTok star known for posting daily videos of herself playing the web-based word game Wordle and sharing the correct answers with her followers and fans of the game. Her dailyxsav TikTok has over 1.8 million followers. 

Before Fame

She posted her first TikTok in January 2022. In the video, she plays a round of Wordle. 

Trivia

She posts selfies, pictures of her loved ones, and life updates on her savdelullo Instagram. One of her most popular videos features a race to make cupcakes with a friend. It has garnered over 3 million views. 

Family Life

She has a sister. Her golden retriever Princess passed away in the Summer of 2022. 

Associated With

In February 2022, she posted a TikTok about the Taylor Swift version of Wordle that came out. She has collaborated with other Wordle players such as Michael DiCostanzo, Cam2r, and Kenny Haller.
