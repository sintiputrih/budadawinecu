Who Is Chase Young?, Age, Weight, Height, Career, Family, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Who Is Chase Young?, Age, Weight, Height, Career, Family, Net Worth: Born on April 14, 1999, Chase Young is an American football defensive end.

He developed a passion for playing American football at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after defensive ends

At this time of writing (Wednesday, November 1, 2023), Young is a defensive end for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League.

He played college football at Ohio State University for the Buckeyes. For his impressive performance, Young declared for the 2020 NFL Draft

Following that season, he was considered by many to be its best overall prospect before being selected second overall by the Washington Football Team.

His 2021 season was seen as a disappointment after he recorded just 1.5 sacks before suffering a major knee injury.

During the 2021 season, Young had 21 tackles, 1.5 sacks, and two forced fumbles before tearing his anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) in his right knee.

Young was placed on the physically unable to perform list at the start of training camp in 2022. He returned to the active roster on November 21, 2022.

He was the honorary pace car driver for the 2022 Federated Auto Parts 400, a NASCAR Cup Series race held in Richmond, Virginia.

In 2023, Young hit the headlines after the Washington Commanders traded him to the San Francisco 49ers for a third-round pick.

The defensive end was traded to the San Francisco 49ers on Tuesday, October 31, 2023, at the NFL trade deadline

Young would have been a free agent after the season. Washington declined to pick up his fifth-year option in the offseason.

It would have counted $17.452 million against the 2024 salary cap but also would have been guaranteed money.

Young pursued a major in criminology at Ohio State after being inspired by his father and several of his uncles and cousins, who have all worked in law enforcement.

During his time with the Buckeyes, he was nicknamed “The Predator” because his dreadlocks resembled Predators from the Predator franchise.

Young is represented by Klutch Sports Group (a sports agency) and serves as their football ambassador for Klutch Athletics (a sportswear brand).

He has appeared in advertisements for Chipotle, Under Armour, and eBay, as well as on episodes of The Shop and Celebrity Family Feud
