Career

Niharika Konidela started her career as a theatre artist and acted in several plays. In her initial days, she also worked at a coffee shop to gain some experience of earning. She made her acting debut with her appearance in the famous dance reality show “Dhee Juniors” on ETV Telugu channel in 2013 as an anchor.

[quads id=5]

After that, she made her debut on the big screen with her appearance in the film “Oka Manasu” in 2006. She also owns a production house named “Pink Elephant Pictures” and produce two web series titled “Nanna Koochi” and “Muddapappu Avakai” and also acted in them. Both the series gained huge popularity and appreciation from viewers as well as critics.

She has made her appearances in many Tamil and Telugu movies like “Oru Nalla Naal Paathu Solren” in 2018, “Happy Wedding” in 2018, “Suryakantham” in 2019, and “Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy” in 2019. She has also appeared in the web series “Madhouse” in 2019.
