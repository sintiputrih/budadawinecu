Kim Kardashian Looks Gorgeous With Blue Contacts: Photos

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Wolfie

A photo posted by Kim Kardashian West (@kimkardashian) on Feb 15, 2015 at 8:50pm PST

Just, whoa! Kim Kardashian stunned her fans when she tried out blue contacts on Sunday, Feb. 15, following her husband Kanye West’s trippy Saturday Night Live 40th Anniversary special performance.

“Wolfie,” Kardashian captioned a photo of herself looking exotic with piercing blue eyes. North West’s mom also shared a photo of West also rocking blue contacts. “Wolves,” she captioned the close-up snap of her hubby, referring to his newly debuted track. 

Wolves

A photo posted by Kim Kardashian West (@kimkardashian) on Feb 15, 2015 at 8:46pm PST

West rocked similar contacts during his performance on Sunday night, during which the rapper performed a medley of his songs including hit “Jesus Walks,” “Only One” and “Wolves” with Sia and Vic Mensa.

“Iconic moment!!! Kanye SNL,” Kardashian gushed on Instagram following his innovative performance, during which the rapper sang while laying flat on the ground with a microphone extended over him.
