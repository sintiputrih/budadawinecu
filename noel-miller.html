Career

Earlier Work & Tiny Meat Gang

He started his career by working as a website designer at a company called Fullscreen. While working at Fullscreen, he made Cody Ko who was a software engineer there. He became famous when he shot and uploaded a short clip of himself on YouTube saying:

Ay. What’s up? It’s ya boy, uh, Skinny P***s”

Fame came to him when the duo came together for ‘That’s Cringe’ commentary series of Cody Ko.

Noel Miller and Cody Ko in That’s Cringe

He has been hosting the podcast ‘Tiny Meat Gang’ (TMG) since 2017 with Cody Ko.

They have performed in venues across the US in shows and events such as Just for Laughs, Outside Lands and the Nashville Comedy Festival. He made his acting debut with the TV series ‘The Real Bros of Simi Valley’ (2020) as ‘Mike Schaffer.’

The Real Bros of Simi Valley (2020)

He has written, produced, edited, and directed podcasts for many episodes of ‘Tiny Meat Gang.’ He has written, directed, produced, and edited the short film Suki (2019).

As a Musical Artist

He has released many songs as a part of TMG. A few of his songs are: Keep Ya Dick Fat, Super Xan, Clout, Stay Safe, Knock It off, No Flex, Walk Man, and Broke Bitch.

He released an album as a part of TMG titled Bangers & Ass (2017).

He has collaborated with other artists as well, which includes American musician Blackbear, and internet personality and musician Rynx.
