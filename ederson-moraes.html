Early Life

Ederson Moraes was born on 17 August 1993 and his age is currently 30 years old. He hails from Osasco, Brazil, and his zodiac sign is Leo. Moreover, his full name is Ederson Santana de Moraes. He holds dual nationality of Brazilian and Portuguese. He acquired the latter in the year 2016.

Ederson Moraes – Net Worth 2024

This well-known footballer has earned a decent amount of money throughout his career. Moving on, this football goalkeeper has a net worth of $16 million US dollars as of 2022. Other than this, there is no information about his salary, cars, houses, asset values, endorsements, etc.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Ederson Moraes is a footballer. People recognize him for being the goalkeeper for Manchester City a Premier League club as well as the Brazil national team. Additionally, this player is the Guinness World Recorder for “Longest Football Drop Kick”. He achieved this record after he kicked the ball 75.35 meters or 247 feet 2 inches across the ground at the Etihad Campus. He made this record on 10 May 2018. Moreover, he also won the Premier League Golden Glove in the year 2019–20 as well as 2020–21. While writing this biography, this personality is the one holding the title.

Caption: Ederson Moraes celebrates the win with his team. Source: Instagram

Talking about the beginning of his career, this footballer started his career at São Paulo in the year 2008. This was before joining the Portuguese side of Benfica a year later. On the Portuguese side, this footballer spent two seasons. Then in the year 2012, this young footballer transferred from Ribeirão to Primeira Liga side Rio Ave. Subsequently, the footballer became a regular starter.

More Details

In the year 2015, this footballer rejoined Benfica and was assigned to the reserves. This was before his debut for the 1st team. With Benfica, the footballer won 4 major titles in two seasons. For the 2017–18 season, this young footballer joined the English club Manchester City. Joining the City for £35 million, this player became the most expensive goalkeeper of all time in terms of the nominal value of pound sterling at the time of his transfer.

Moreover, the player then won the Premier League as well as the EFL Cup in his 1st season in England. Likewise, this player also won a domestic treble the next season.

Caption: Ederson Moraes signing with Manchester City. Source: Instagram

Furthermore, this player made his senior debut for Brazil in the year 2017. This was after previously being capped at the under-23 level. Moreover, this player was chosen for Brazil’s squad for the 2018 World Cup. He was also chosen for both the 2019 and 2021 Copa América. Playing in the 2019 tournament, this goalkeeper won the game.

Relationship Status

Ederson Moraes is a happily married guy. His wife’s name is Lais Moraes and the two tied the knot in April 2014. Together, the lovers have three children their names are Yasmin, Henrique, and Laurinha.
