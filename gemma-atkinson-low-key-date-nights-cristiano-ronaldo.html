Gemma Atkinson stuns fans with truth about romance with Cristiano Ronaldo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 5, 2024

The former couple dated in 2007

    Gemma Atkinson has been happily engaged to partner Gorka Marquez since last year and together they share one daughter, two-year-old Mia.
