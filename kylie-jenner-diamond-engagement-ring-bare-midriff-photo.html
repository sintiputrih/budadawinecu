Kylie Jenner Sparks Engagement Rumors by Wearing a Diamond Ring on Her Left Hand

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Kylie Jenner is, once again, helping to generate engagement rumors by wearing a huge diamond ring on her left hand. Posing on Instagram in an all-white ensemble, which also shows off her toned midriff, the major new diamond is clearly visible, and it's easy to see why it's starting rumors. The Daily Mail has even more photos of the ring, although no-one's confirmed, as yet, whether it's from Travis Scott.

Recently, Scott created engagement rumors by saying that he plans to propose to Jenner soon. Speaking to Rolling Stone last month, Scott revealed, "I need her with me to operate. She’s that one." He added, "We’ll get married soon. I just gotta sturdy up—I gotta propose in a fire way.”

Having already welcomed their first baby—daughter Stormi—together last year, Jenner and Scott certainly seem as though they're extremely committed to one another and their family. They even reportedly purchased a mansion together back in October 2018, for around $13.45 million, with People reporting that Scott and Jenner split the bill.
