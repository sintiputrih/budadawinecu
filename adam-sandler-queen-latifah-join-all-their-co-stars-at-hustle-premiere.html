Adam Sandler &amp; Queen Latifah Join All Their Co-Stars at Hustle Premiere

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Adam Sandler and Queen Latifah‘s new movie, Hustle, is about to debut in theaters!

The two stars, along with cast mates Juancho Hernangomez, Jaleel White, Maria Botto, Kenny Smith, Heidi Gardner, Jordan Hull, and producer LeBron James, stepped out for the premiere of the film on Wednesday night (June 1) in Los Angeles.

Karrueche Tran, Usher, LaKeith Stanfield, Khleo Thomas, Paris Berelc, Luenell, and Jon Lovitz were among the celebrity guests there.

Here’s what Hustle is about: After discovering a once-in-a-lifetime player (Hernangomez) with a rocky past abroad, a down on his luck basketball scout (Sandler) takes it upon himself to bring the phenom to the States without his team’s approval. Against the odds, they have one final shot to prove they have what it takes to make it in the NBA.

The film will be out in select theaters on Friday, June 3, and then debut on Netflix next week on June 8.
