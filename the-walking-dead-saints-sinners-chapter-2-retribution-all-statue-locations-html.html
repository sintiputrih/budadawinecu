All Locations of Statues

Resting Place

The angel statue is located behind the crypt that you use to trade messages with May in Chapter 1.

The demon statue is just past the gate on the left where you are first introduced to flares in the beginning of the game.

Old Town

The angel status is located behind a desk in an alleyway in the lower center of the map.

The demon statue is located upstairs in the blue house, behind a sofa just inside the hole in the wall where you can climb up.

The Shallows

The angel statue is located in the attic of the blue mansion

The demon statue is located in the crawl space below in the same house.

Via Carolla

The angel statue is located in a first floor closet cabinet on the north side of the blue building.

The devil statue is tricky to find. It is located at the back of a semi trailer in a secret area south of the blue house. To get there, start at the southmost end of Congenial street and climb over a trash bin on the East side proceed through the narrow corridor between the fence and house.

Memorial Lane

The angel statue is located in the upstairs storage room of the red building where a dummy resides.

The demon statue is located in the alley just north of the red house behind a sofa.

The Ward

The angel statue is located in the warehouse on the East side of the Nile Shipping company.

The demon statue is located in the storage room after the Axeman fight and can be picked up after that point in the story or just after the Axeman fight if done at night.

Bastion

The angel statue is located behind trash bags in the yellow building

The devil statue is located in the secret room in basement of the blue house.

Bywater

The angel is located in a hidden attic area accessible from the garage of the auto shop; just follow the bloody handholds.

The demon statue is located in the back of an open semi trailer in the middle of the lumberyard.

Rampart

The angel statue is located in the Art room filled with easels.

The demon statue is located on top of the room in the Northeast corner of the gym, accessible from handholds at the top of the bleachers.

Hotel E’Claire

The angel statue is located upstairs in the Heads or Tails speakeasy inside the locked room. This can only be accessed during the the Father Carter mission of the game unless you restarted the mission and gathered additional tokens for re-entry later.

The demon statue is hidden in the fountain in the north side of the central courtyard.

Bourbon Street

The angel statue is located in the hidden underground passageway from the Tiki bar to the radio station.

The demon statue is located in the green building, downstairs beneath the bar.

Tower

The angel statue is located behind a camouflaged truck at the entrance of the large supply tent in front of the tower.

The demon statue is located at the bottom of the elevator shaft at the left side of the hospital lobby. After the final Tower mission, the hospital is normally blocked off if you attempt to go there post-game at night, but it is possible to climb over the outhouses and fence next to the Tower base to reach the Hospital side to fetch the statue.
