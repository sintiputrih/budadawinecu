Jessi Colter Net Worth 2024

Introduction

Jessi Colter, born Mirriam Johnson, is a renowned American country music artist who is best known for her collaboration with her late husband, Waylon Jennings, and for her 1975 country-pop crossover hit “I’m Not Lisa.” Colter’s career in the music industry has spanned over five decades, and she has made significant contributions to the country and outlaw country music genres. This article will delve into the projected net worth of Jessi Colter in 2024, taking into account her earnings from her music career, royalties, and other ventures.

Jessi Colter’s Music Career

Jessi Colter started her music career in the late 1960s, initially recording two singles for the minor record label Jamie. However, her breakthrough came in the mid-1970s when she released “I’m Not Lisa,” which topped the country charts and reached the top 5 on the pop charts. Since then, Colter has released numerous albums and singles, both as a solo artist and in collaboration with other artists, most notably her late husband, Waylon Jennings.

Album Sales and Royalties

Album sales and royalties form a significant part of Jessi Colter’s net worth. Her albums, especially those released during the peak of her career, have sold millions of copies worldwide. Additionally, as a songwriter, Colter earns royalties from her songs whenever they are played on radio stations, streamed online, or used in movies and TV shows.

Concerts and Tours

Concerts and tours are another major source of income for musicians, and Jessi Colter is no exception. Over the years, she has performed at numerous venues across the United States and around the world, earning substantial income from ticket sales and merchandise.

Jessi Colter’s Other Ventures

Apart from her music career, Jessi Colter has also ventured into other areas that have contributed to her net worth. These include book writing and acting.

Book Writing

In 2017, Colter released an autobiography titled “An Outlaw and a Lady: A Memoir of Music, Life with Waylon, and the Faith that Brought Me Home.” The book, which details her life and career, has been well-received and has contributed to her earnings.

Acting

Colter has also dabbled in acting, appearing in a few films and TV shows. While her acting career has not been as successful as her music career, it has nonetheless added to her overall net worth.

Investments and Assets

Charitable Work

Projected Net Worth in 2024
