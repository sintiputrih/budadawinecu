Jessie J Shaves Her Head Bald for Charity: Picture

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 23, 2024

Jessie J's trademark bob is gone, gone, gone! The British pop star, 24, made good on a recent promise to go practically bald, shaving off her sleek brunette locks on live TV for the 25th annual Comic Relief fundraiser, which aired on BBC1 in the UK. The "Domino" singer did the deed when the special, part of "Red Nose Day" in England, aired on Friday (March 15)

"I did it! Thank you to everyone who is donating!" the chanteuse (full name: Jessica Ellen Cornish) tweeted. And her efforts were worth it: With One Direction, Simon Cowell and other British stars taking part, the Comic Relief special raised 75 million British pounds — or $113 million in U.S. currency — towards various causes in the UK and Africa.
