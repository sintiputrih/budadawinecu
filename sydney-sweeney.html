Sydney Sweeney is a well-known American actress. Sydney Sweeney is most recognized for her roles as Emaline Addario in the online series ‘Everything Sucks!’ and Haley Caren in ‘In The Vault’.

 Early Life and Childhood

Sydney Sweeney was born in Spokane, Washington, USA on September 12, 1997. She is 26 years old. Scott Sweeney, her father, and Lisa Mudd Sweeney, her mother, gave birth to her. Trent Sweeney, her younger brother, is also an actor.

She is of White ancestry and has American nationality. Her astrological sign is Virgo. Her religious beliefs, however, are unknown. In terms of schooling, she holds a bachelor’s degree in entrepreneurship.

She is an MMA fighter who began training when she was 14 years old. When she was in high school, she participated in grappling.

Professional Life

On October 12, 2009, Sydney Sweeney debuted as Little Girl in Episode 4 of Season 4 of the NBC-aired American science fiction television drama series, ‘Heroes,’ titled ‘Hysterical Blindness.

She appeared as Dani Forester in a single episode of the popular American police procedural crime drama television series ‘Criminal Minds the same year. Similarly, she played Lisa in the 2010 film ‘ZMD: Zombies of Mass Destruction.’

She has also played Samantha Wright in the short thriller ‘Takeo,’ Sarah Detzer in the American drama ‘The Opium Eater,’ and Young Alice in the supernatural horror film ‘The Ward’. That same year, she played Lost Girl in the short film ‘Night Blind’.

Likewise, she starred as Kayla Edwards in the premiere episode of the American police procedural drama television series ‘Chase,’ which aired on NBC on September 20, 2010.

She also appeared as Girl in the episode ‘How Much Is That Liam in the Window’ of the American adolescent drama television series 90210′ that same year.

She appeared as Kelsey Vargas in the episode ‘Swords and Magic’ of the comedic television series ‘Kickin’ It’ in 2011.

That same year, she played Izzy Fishman in the television film ‘The Bling Ring’. She played Emily in ‘Spiders 3D,’ an American 3D science fiction monster horror film released on February 8, 2013.

Moreover, she played Annie in ‘Angels in Stardust,’ an American coming-of-age comedy-drama film released on February 21, 2014.

The following year, she appeared as Erin Weaver in the ABC-televised American medical drama television series ‘Grey’s Anatomy in the episode ‘Don’t Let’s Start.

After 2015

She made her cinematic debut as Julia in ‘The Martial Arts Kid’ on September 18, 2015. She also played Emma in the film ‘Stolen From Suburbia,’ Leah in the short film ‘Love Made Visibly,’ and Little Janey Hutchins in the short film ‘The Unborn that same year.

Similarly, she played Kelsey Connors in the thriller film ‘Cassidy Way,’ which was released on February 23, 2016. She also appeared in the action-horror film ‘The Horde,’ which was released on May 6, 2016.

In the romantic comedy film ‘Vikes,’ she portrayed Ida. She also played Sam in the comedy film ‘Dead Ant and Young Paige in the short film ‘It Happened Again Last Night’ that same year.

In 2017, she made her debut as Haley Caren in the online series ‘In the Vault.’ She appeared in seven episodes of the show. She appeared as Female Student in the American sitcom TV series ‘The Middle’.

There she played Willa in the American adolescent drama mystery-thriller television series ‘Pretty Little Liars,’ both in the same year.

In that same year, she voiced Madeline Rayon in the animated TV series ‘Monster School Animation’ in the episode titled ‘Welcome to Monster School”.
