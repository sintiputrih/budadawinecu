Meet Rodrigo Reyes! His Age, Net Worth, Height

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 3, 2024

Rodrigo Reyes who already has ties with a few cast members of Southern Charm is sharing frames with them in season 9. The show premiered in September 2023. So, what is he going to bring to the table and all the drama? Get a closer look at his personal and professional life via this article here.

This article is about his age, net worth, partner, and more.

Rodrigo Reyes On Southern Charm

Rodrigo Reyes is one of the newcomers on Bravo’s season 9 of Southern Charm. The recently debuted on the channel on 14 September 2023 and was available on Peacock for streaming the day after.

In the dramatic teaser, Madison LeCroy and Austen Kroll, two returning Charm-ers, argue about the latter’s alleged hookup with Taylor Ann Green. Taylor’s possible liaison with Austen also seems to have an impact on her friendships with Olivia Flowers, a close friend, and Shep Rose, an ex-boyfriend. While everything is going on, Craig Conover must also consider other issues, such as how he will spend the rest of his life with his girlfriend Paige DeSorbo. Additionally returning to the cast are Leva Bonaparte and Venita Aspen, who will be joined by three new cast members.

They are Rodrigo, Jarrett Thomas, and Rod Razavi.

Although Rodrigo is new to Southern Charm, the rest of the group has known him for a long time. Rodrigo serves as a voice of reason and keeps the group “grounded,” according to his Bravo bio. Rodrigo is currently engaged, unlike his castmates Taylor, Shep, Austen, Olivia, Jarrett, and Rod. Tyler has been his boyfriend for eight years.

Rodrigo’s official Bravo bio read: “Rodrigo is an interior designer in a long-term relationship with his boyfriend, Tyler. He has been friends with the Charmers for many years and helps to keep them grounded.”

After the show’s premiere, Rodrigo took to his IG and wrote, “Wish Me Luck Mañana. .#charleston  #southerncharm  #bravo  #bravotv #greenscreen #mexicano #instagay.”

What Is Rodrigo Reyes Dating Status?

Rodrigo Reyes is in a committed relationship with his long-time partner Tyler Dugas. As of 2023, they have been in a relationship for 8 years now as they recently celebrated their anniversary in July 2023.

Rodrigo wrote on the occasion, “8 years, multiple trips around the world, and 4 dogs later… Happy Anniversary my love @tsdugey.”

During this nearly decade-long relationship, they have seen many places together including Cabos, Mexico, and Greece.

Born in 1988, Tyler is 35 years old now in 2023. He is the son of Carolyn Dugas and Stephen Dugas and has a brother named Cory Dugas. He is working as a physical therapist at HCA. He graduated from University of St Augustine for Health Sciences and Florida State University.

Rodrigo Reyes Age

As of September 2023, Rodrigo Reyes’s current age is 39 years old. 1984 is his birth year.

Rodrigo Reyes Family

Rodrigo Reyes comes from a close-knit Mexican family. Mary Reyes and Rodrigo Reyes are his parents who are residents of Coolidge, Arizona.

Rodrigo has featured his father several times on his socials. “#HappyFathersDay to the original Rodrigo Reyes. Love you Pa! #mexican #cowboy,” Father’s Day message for his dad from 2019 reads.

The reality star’s mom is Mexican as he states in his Mother’s Day Message from 2020 where he writes, “My Mexican Hiking Queen looking good for her mid-70s. Happy Mother’s Day ❤️.” He proudly himself as a mama’s boy and Mary is still her main lady.

The family also comprises his siblings. He has a sister named Reyna Cooper. His other sister is called Maricela Reyes. Marciela went to Central Arizona College.

Rodrigo not only has sisters but also has one brother named Rafael Reyes. Rafael is working as an Enterprise Account Executive – Public Sector at Datadog. He is married to his wife Katie Reyes.

Rodrigo is also related to a few other German Reyes from Eloy, Arizona. Another family member of Rodrigo is called Roberto Reyes.

How Much Is Rodrigo Reyes Net Worth?

With a thriving career as an “Interior Décorateur and Floral Designer”, Rodrigo Reyes has been able to build a net worth above $1 million. He runs his own interior designing business called RAR Designs. Connect to his business via his website and Instagram.

According to his LinkedIn, Rodrigo previously worked as a design assistant to Matthew Mclaughlin Design for four years from 2008 to 2012. During this period, he was responsible for providing “design and administrative support to the interior designer,” assisting “with paint color selection and furniture selections for several residential installation projects and spatial organization.”

Rodrigo also performed “physical installation at job sites to achieve the client’s desired results.” He also cataloged “furniture inventory and pricing for local antique mall space.”

An attendee of Arizona State University, Rodrigo graduated in 2008. He studied design management there. While in the ASU, he also participated in the ASU chapter of the Public Relations Student Society of America. He also participated in the Athena program for students of architecture, interior design, graphic design, and urban planning.”
