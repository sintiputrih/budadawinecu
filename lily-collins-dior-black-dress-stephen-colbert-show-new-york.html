Lily Collins' latest French It-girl outfits are straight out of the Emily in Paris playbook

    In the case of Lily Collins, life certainly imitates art.

    The star of Netflix's hit showwore an ensemble worthy of a French film star for her appearance on Good Morning America in New York, and we’re still not over it.

    MORE: Lily Collins is Emily's style opposite at Emily in Paris premiere

    RELATED: Emily in Paris: Lily Collins shows off new statement knit in season three trailer

    Lily wore Fendi AW22

    The 32-year-old actress donned an outfit from Fendi’s Autumn Winter 2022 runway show which consisted of Look 11’s mini jumpsuit which she paired with Look 13’s blazer. She paired the outfit with sheer black tights and vertiginous platform heels, with her trademark fringe and gold Cartier hoops the entire look was the picture of Parisian It-girl understated elegance. Despite her on screen character Emily Cooper (styled by Patricia Field of Sex and the City Fame) prefering bold colours and bright clashing patterns, Lily proved she knows how to truly master that certain je ne sais quoi of French style. The looks were put together by stylist duo Mariel Haenn and Rob Rob Zangardi.

    Lily looked like a French film star in Dior

    The A-Lister stylists also crafted the look she wore later on the day to visit the 'The Late Show With Stephen Colbert' at the Ed Sullivan Theater. The outfit couldn’t have been more iconically retro-French silver screen siren if they tried; a netted veil headpiece, plus Dior dress? Magnifique. The look was finished with opaque tights and Christian Louboutin heels teamed with Cartier’s Clash de Cartier necklace. Lily took to her Instagram to share photos of the Dior look, and although she might be one of Netflix’s leading ladies she isn’t averse to tuning into other shows from the streaming giants saying “Channeling Wednesday on a Monday.” Clearly she’s not only been taking style cues from the city of love.

    Who is Lily Collins' stylist?

    Rob Zangardi and Mariel Haenn have been styling Lily since at least 2017. The talented duo have been behind many of the most iconic style moments in fashion (including dressing Rihanna for the music video and Jennifer Lopez for the Super Bowl) Their A-list clientele includes Gwen Stefani, Hailee Steinfeld, Camila Cabello, J.Lo and Mila Kunis.
