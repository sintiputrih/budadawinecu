Vladimir Guerrero was born on February 19, 1975 in Don Gregorio, Dominican Republic. He was a 9-time MLB All-Star who had an impressive career, hitting 449 home runs with the Montreal Expos and Los Angeles Angels. In 2004, he was named American League MVP and led the Angels to an AL West Championship.

9-time MLB All-Star who hit 449 home runs throughout his career, mostly for the Montreal Expos and Los Angeles Angels. He was named 2004 American League MVP while leading the Angels to an AL West Championship.

Before Fame

Growing up, he developed strong hands after bringing in uncooperative bulls at the end of the day on his father's farm.

Trivia

Family Life

His brothers Wilton and Cristian both played baseball professionally as well. His son Vladimir Guerrero Jr. also became a major leaguer. 

Associated With
