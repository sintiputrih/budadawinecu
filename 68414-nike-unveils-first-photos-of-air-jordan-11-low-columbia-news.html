Nike Unveils First Photos Of Air Jordan 11 Low Columbia

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001

Back in August news broke that Nike would be retroing the popular “Columbia” colorway of the Air Jordan 11 low. The shoe first released in 2001, so Jordan Brand just missed the 15th anniversary. Now we have official photos of the 2017 retro for your viewing pleasure (albeit the kids’ version).

The original “Columbia” blue has been renamed “University Blue,” but the shade of the blue is the same. The shoe retains it’s mostly white upper with the aformentioned blue across the patent leather and accents. The icy outsole has the slightly blue hue Jordan has been using on their shoes recently instead of the true clear version on the OGs.

The sneaker will retail for $175 in men’s sizes, but the prices for the kids’ sizes hasn’t been released yet. There has been no confirmation on a release date, but the May 2017 rumors have persisted. Is this one a cop next year?
